Privacy Policy — Make Grok Your New Tab

Last updated: 2026-07-10
Publisher: davcall
Extension: Make Grok Your New Tab (Microsoft Edge)

Summary

Make Grok Your New Tab replaces Microsoft Edge’s default new-tab page with Grok AI (https://grok.com) by default, or another start URL that you choose. The extension is designed to keep your data local to your browser.

Data we collect

We do not collect, sell, or transmit personal information to our servers. There is no analytics backend, account system, or remote telemetry operated by the publisher for this extension.

Data stored on your device

The extension uses the browser’s chrome.storage.sync API to store only:

  • Your chosen start page URL
  • Your preferred open mode (redirect or iframe)

This data stays in your browser profile (and may sync across your signed-in Microsoft Edge / Chromium profiles if browser sync is enabled). The publisher cannot access it.

Permissions

  • storage — required to save and load your start URL and open-mode preference.

The extension does not request browsing-history, tabs, cookies, or host-wide webRequest permissions.

Network activity

When you open a new tab, the extension may navigate to (or embed) the URL you configured. That navigation is a normal browser request to the site you chose and is subject to that site’s own privacy policy (for example, grok.com). The extension itself does not inject tracking scripts or send data to the publisher.

Children’s privacy

The extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.
