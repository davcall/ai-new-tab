Source: github.com/davcall/ai-new-tab

Summary

This extension replaces Microsoft Edge’s default new-tab page with a preset AI site (Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT, Grok) or a custom URL you choose. The extension is designed to keep your data local to your browser.

Data we collect

We do not collect, sell, or transmit personal information to our servers. There is no analytics backend, account system, or remote telemetry operated by the publisher for this extension.

Data stored on your device

The extension uses the browser’s chrome.storage.sync API to store only:

  • Which preset you selected (Grok, Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or Custom)
  • Your custom URL (if Custom is selected)
  • Your preferred open mode (redirect or iframe)

This data stays in your browser profile (and may sync across your signed-in browser profiles if sync is enabled). The publisher cannot access it.

Permissions

  • storage — required to save and load your preset and URL preferences.

Network activity

When you open a new tab, the extension navigates to (or embeds) the site you configured. That is a normal browser request to that site and is subject to that site’s privacy policy (for example grok.com, claude.ai, chatgpt.com, gemini.google.com). The extension does not inject tracking scripts or send data to the publisher.

Supply-chain transparency

Release packages are built on GitHub Actions with public checksums and attestations. Verification steps: VERIFY.md.
